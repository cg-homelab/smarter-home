# Code of Conduct

## Our Goal

This project aims to be open, friendly, and useful for everyone. We want contributors and users to feel welcome, respected, and safe when participating.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before jumping to conclusions.
- Focus on ideas and code, not personal criticism.
- Give feedback kindly and accept feedback gracefully.
- Help keep discussions inclusive and on-topic.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable in project spaces:

- Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks, insults, or sustained hostile behavior.
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or spam.
- Sharing someone else's private information without permission.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels where this project is represented.

## Reporting and Enforcement

If you experience or witness a problem, contact the maintainers through a listed project channel. If you are not comfortable reporting publicly, contact a maintainer directly when possible.

Maintainers will review reports in good faith, respond as fairly as possible, and may take actions such as requesting changes, removing content, or temporarily or permanently restricting participation for repeated or severe violations.

## Final Notes

This is a community project. Please be kind, practical, and collaborative.
